SAVE THE DATE - May 20, 7pm PST

Given only a screenshot, Code in the Dark is a competition where frontend development meets smoke machines, lasers and sweet beats by DJ Bogue! Whether you're a full-time dev, happily self-employed or a student getting to grips with it all - we want YOU to join in Spokane's first Code in the Dark event.

The rules of the competition are simple

  • One screenshot, view it as much as you want
  • 15 minutes per round of coding (no previews, you're in the dark!)
  • Public previews when the time's up
  • One Champion!

Attendees are welcome to cheer on their friends and watch them in real-time via mirrored screens.

The winner of each heat is decided by the audience. The competition continues until there's only one contestant left — the Champion of the Dark.

You don't have to be a developer to have fun at Code in the Dark, everyone is welcome, contestant or not.
